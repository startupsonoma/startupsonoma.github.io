Startup Sonoma

Moving from Idea to Product in Sonoma County

Helping Sonoma County Entrepreneurs to start companies by having peer support as well as getting the community services involved around support the entrepreneurs.

A lot of these ideas are lifted from the book Startup Communities.

Talks

  • Birthing an Idea
  • Lean Methodology
  • CRMs
  • Accounting
  • Incorporation
  • Landing Site
  • MailChimp
  • CRM

Partners

The primary partners are entrepreneurs helping other entrepreneurs. However, we should get the community involved in this as well. The least useful thing to worry about is VCs as VCs will come where there is innovation.

  • Accountants
  • Lawyers
  • Web Designers
  • Governments
  • Maker Space
  • Shared Space
  • Mentors
  • Large Companies
  • VC
  • Community College
  • Sonoma State
  • School District
  • Libraries

Ideas

  • [ ] Young Entrepreneurs (Schools)
    • Teaching entrepreneurship at schools
    • Lifting Latinos, Blacks and women by teaching them entrepreneurial skills isntead of working for the man.
  • [ ] Weekly or Biweekly Coffee Meetups for entrepreneurs to throw around ideas.
  • [ ] Office Hours
    • [ ] Get help with different things and maybe get some free advice for entrepreneurs just starting out.
  • [ ] Bring different service providers together such as lawyer, accountants, etc.
  • [ ] Talks aimed at starting companies. Basic accounting, marketing, etc.
  • [ ] Mentorships
  • [ ] Hackathons
  • [ ] Startup Weekends. Build an app or startup in a weekend.

Some tenants

  • No idea is stupid, they should be validated quickly.
  • Reduce wasted time and resources.
  • Failure of an idea is not a failure of the individual.
  • Give before you get.
  • Entrepreneurship is the future of America.
  • Entrepreneurship is Community Service.
